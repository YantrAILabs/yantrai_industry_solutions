The Intelligence
Layer Explained.

Traditional software waits to be told what to do. AI agents watch, learn, and act — continuously. Think of it less like a dashboard and more like hiring a team of tireless, hyper-observant analysts who never sleep, never blink, and escalate the right thing to the right person in seconds.

A simple analogy: A spreadsheet is a tool — it needs a human to enter data, interpret it, and decide. An AI agent is more like a seasoned site manager who watches everything at once, flags anomalies the moment they appear, and hands you a decision — not a data dump. The difference isn't incremental.
