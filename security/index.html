Use Case 01

24/7 Intelligent Security Monitoring.

Maintain continuous awareness across your entire site with AI-powered monitoring. Instantly detect emerging threats — from firearms and perimeter breaches to suspicious exits — and respond before incidents escalate.

Use Case 02

Operational Efficiency at Scale.

Automate routine security workflows and maximize operational productivity. AI filters out 95% of false alarms, allowing teams to focus on real incidents. Intelligent video analysis speeds up investigations and delivers answers in minutes, not hours.

Use Case 03

Proactive Threat Response.

Detect early indicators of risk — including sudden egress, tailgating, and unusual activity — in real time. AI eliminates low-priority alerts and highlights genuine threats so security teams can act 10× faster and stop incidents before they unfold.

Use Case 04

Data-Driven Security Decisions.

Unlock operational insights across your security infrastructure. Use analytics on tailgating incidents, camera coverage gaps, and access control violations to refine processes, improve coverage, and strategically deploy teams.
